Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Adaa collects only what's needed to run the app. We never sell your data. We never show ads. Your health data stays yours.

1 Who We Are

Adaa is a personal health and performance tracking app. When we say "Adaa," "we," "us," or "our," we mean the team building and operating this service.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@adaa.health

2 What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account info: email address (when you sign in with Google or magic link)
  • Health data: weight, calorie logs, exercise records, habit completions, step counts, water intake, and notes — all entered by you
  • Onboarding data: height, age, gender, activity level, and goal (for TDEE calculation only)
  • Device/session info: browser type and language preference (stored locally)

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or any data beyond what's listed above.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide and sync your personal tracking data across devices
  • Calculate your TDEE and personalized calorie goal
  • Display your progress history and weekly insights
  • Authenticate your identity via Supabase (our secure backend)
  • Send authentication emails (magic links) when you request them

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than making the app work for you.

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with industry-standard encryption. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

If you use the app without signing in, your data is stored only on your device (localStorage) and is never sent to our servers.

5 Third Parties

We work with a small number of trusted services to run Adaa:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Your data lives in their secure cloud.
  • Google — used only if you choose "Sign in with Google." We receive only your email address.
  • Vercel — hosts the web app. They do not access your personal data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third party, ever.

6 Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: you can view all your data at any time inside the app
  • Delete: you can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Portability: your data is human-readable and tied to your account
  • Opt-out: stop using the app at any time — no lock-in

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@adaa.health

7 Cookies & Local Storage

Adaa uses browser localStorage to store your session token, language preference, app data (when not signed in), and onboarding state. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

8 Children's Privacy

Adaa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we'll notify you within the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10 Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? We're happy to help.
